## 📋 PRD: Anniversary Wrapped — v3 (Final)

**Product:** A mobile-first, slide-by-slide interactive web experience gifted to a partner on an 11th anniversary. Spotify Wrapped meets a personal love letter — warm, romantic, photo-rich, and partially interactive.

---

### Design Principles
- **Mobile-first**, full-screen slides, tap to advance
- **Warm and romantic** palette — creams, blush, warm gold
- **Photo-forward** — photos are a feature, not decoration
- **Interactive moments** — she picks, guesses, and builds alongside the experience
- **Playful but heartfelt** — humour in the hard stuff, sincerity in the big stuff

---
